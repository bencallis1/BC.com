My Method

Get Inspired

Before I can start any project I need to get inspired. I'll throw on some good music and go on a hike or hit the slopes with my wife(we have two kids so that can be hard sometimes). My family is a huge motivator for me, I try to always remember who I'm doing it all for. I am constantly researching new design trends and framewaorks so I can stay fresh on what's current.

Be Passionate

My best work comes from projects that I can be passionate about. For me it's important to remember why I started developing and that was to create products that were going to increase the quality of life for people. So whether it's programming robots or fine tuning motors I need to see some value in it. And yes making cars faster does increase quality of life and everyone loves robots.

Be Positive

This is the most important thing for me. I have to surround myself with posistive people who are constantly trying to be better. I'm a positive energetic person so I try surround myself with good people that don't mind having a good time. Work doesn't have to be a grind.
